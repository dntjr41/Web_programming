About why I went on a trip

I thought a lot about my career in the army.
I needed time to find my self, and I decided to go on a trip
after I was discharged. I decided to spend about half a year
traveling because I thought I would be short for a month or two.

How I collected my travel expenses

I got a salary of 350,000 won in the military,
but I saved 300,000 won each. As a result, I collected about
5,000,000 won. Also, I worked as a part-time security at a E-mart
and construction site for about four months, and collected 8,000,000 won
and raised a total 13,000,000 won for travel expenses.

About what I got from my trip

I had to decide everything on my own, and in the process it became harder.
As a result, I didn't find the exact answer to my career,
but I learned how to find my way in life.
